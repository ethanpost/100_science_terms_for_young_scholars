## 032_Convection_06.png — Sea breeze and land breeze

**Placement:** End of “Sea Breezes and Land Breezes.”

**Scene:** Two diagrams stacked or side by side.

**Day — Sea breeze**
- Sun over land and water
- Land labeled “heats faster”
- Air rising over land
- Arrow from ocean toward beach: “sea breeze”

**Night — Land breeze**
- Moon or dim sun set
- Land labeled “cools faster”
- Air rising over warmer water
- Arrow from land toward water: “land breeze”

Keep geography simple (strip of land, block of water, stick-figure beach optional).
