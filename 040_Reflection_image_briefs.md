## 040_Reflection_01.png — Reflection (opening)

**Placement:** Top of chapter (after title).

**Labels (optional):** Reflection — light bounces off a surface.

**Caption in chapter:** `![Reflection](040_Reflection_01.png)`

**Note:** Existing art may be a full infographic; for series consistency, a single clear opening scene is enough. Keep or simplify as needed.

## 040_Reflection_02.png — Seeing by reflected light

**Placement:** End of “Seeing by Reflected Light” (after bold rule).

**Scene:** Three-step path left to right:

1. Sun or lamp (labeled **source**)
2. Basketball or backpack (labeled **object** — does not make its own light)
3. Eye (labeled **you see reflected light**)

Arrows: source → object → eye.

**Caption idea:** Seeing by reflected light.

## 040_Reflection_03.png — Law of reflection

**Placement:** End of “The Law of Reflection.”

**Scene:** Flat mirror or smooth surface with:

- **Incident ray** (arrow toward surface)
- **Normal** (dashed perpendicular line)
- **Reflected ray** (arrow away, symmetric)
- Equal angles marked: θᵢ and θᵣ (or “angle of incidence” / “angle of reflection”)

**Formula box (optional):** angle of incidence = angle of reflection

**Caption idea:** The law of reflection.

---

## 040_Reflection_04.png — Regular vs diffuse reflection

**Placement:** End of “Diffuse Reflection.”

**Scene:** Split panel.

| Left — regular | Right — diffuse |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Smooth mirror; parallel incoming rays; parallel outgoing rays; clear upright image | Rough paper or wall; same incoming rays; rays scatter in many directions; no sharp mirror image |

**Labels:** Regular reflection; Diffuse reflection.

**Caption idea:** Regular and diffuse reflection.

---

## 040_Reflection_05.png — Plane mirror and virtual image

**Placement:** End of “Plane Mirrors.”

**Scene:** Object (e.g., water bottle or student silhouette) in front of flat mirror. Solid rays reflect from mirror; dashed extensions behind mirror meet at **virtual image**. Equal distances marked: object distance = image distance.

**Labels:** Real object; Virtual image; Plane mirror.

**Caption idea:** Plane mirror and virtual image.

---

## 040_Reflection_06.png — Concave and convex mirrors

**Placement:** End of “Curved Mirrors.”

**Scene:** Two small panels.

| Concave (curves inward) | Convex (curves outward) |
|-------------------------|-------------------------|
| Spoon bowl side or diagram; rays converge / focus | Spoon back side or diagram; rays spread; wider field of view |

**Labels:** Concave — can focus; Convex — wider view.

Optional tiny use labels: telescope / makeup mirror; bike or store security mirror.

**Caption idea:** Concave and convex mirrors.

---

## 040_Reflection_07.png — Retroreflection and periscope

**Placement:** End of “Retroreflection.”

**Scene:** Split or stacked.

| Top or left | Bottom or right |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Car headlight → bicycle reflector or road sign; arrow returns toward driver (**retroreflection**) | Simple periscope tube with two mirrors; light path over wall into eye |

**Labels:** Retroreflection; Periscope.

**Caption idea:** Retroreflection and periscope.

---

## 040_Reflection_08.png — Glare and water reflection

**Placement:** End of “Reflection in Water.”

**Scene:** Split panel.

| Calm water | Rippled water |
|------------|---------------|
| Clear upside-down tree or sky image; orderly rays | Broken, scattered reflections; many tilted surface bits |

Optional third mini: skier or driver with **glare** from snow or wet road (sunglasses/visor).

**Labels:** Smooth → clear image; Rough/moving → scattered; Glare (optional).

**Caption idea:** Glare and water reflection.
