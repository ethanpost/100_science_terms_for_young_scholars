# 057. Chromosome

## A First Look

Before a cell divides, its DNA must be packed neatly. Chromosomes are the organized packages.

In science, **chromosome** means **a long, organized package of DNA and proteins**. The word may seem simple at first, but it gives scientists a precise way to describe patterns in biology.

## The Main Idea

A helpful way to remember chromosome is to think of it through the phrase **DNA package**. That phrase or image is not perfect, but it gives your mind a handle for the concept before the details become more formal.

Chromosomes carry many genes.

Different species have different numbers of chromosomes.

## Common Misconception

A chromosome is not a single gene; it is a large DNA package containing many genes.

This matters because science rewards careful distinctions. Two ideas can sound similar in ordinary speech but mean different things in science class.
