## 038_Boiling_03.png — Evaporation vs boiling

**Placement:** End of “Boiling and Evaporation” (after the bold rule).

**Scene:** Split panel (similar spirit to `034_Evaporation_03.png`).

| Side | Show | Labels |
|------|------|--------|
| Evaporation | Puddle or cup surface only; quiet wavy lines from top surface | Surface only; below boiling point; quiet |
| Boiling | Pot on burner; bubbles throughout liquid; arrows rising | Throughout liquid; bubbles inside; louder |

**Bottom banner:** Evaporation = surface escape. Boiling = bubbles throughout the liquid.

**Caption idea:** Evaporation vs boiling.
