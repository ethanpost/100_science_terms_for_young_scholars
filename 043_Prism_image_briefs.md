## 043_Prism_03.png — Enter, travel, exit

**Placement:** End of “Refraction in a Prism.”

**Scene:** Side view of prism with three segments of one ray:

1. Entering (bend at entry)
2. Straight through inside glass
3. Exiting (second bend; beam direction shifted)

**Labels:** Enters; inside glass; exits; direction changes.

**Caption idea:** Light enters, travels inside, and exits a prism.

## 043_Prism_04.png — Dispersion

**Placement:** End of “Dispersion.”

**Scene:** Prism with white ray in; fan of colored rays out. Red ray least bent, violet most bent. Optional wall spectrum band.

**Labels:** Red bends less; violet bends more; dispersion.

**Caption idea:** Dispersion: violet bends more than red.

## 043_Prism_05.png — Rainbow in a raindrop

**Placement:** End of “Prisms and Rainbows.”

**Scene:** Single large raindrop cross-section. Sun rays enter → refract → internal reflection → refract out → color spread toward eye. Sun behind viewer (small icon optional).

**Labels:** Refract; reflect inside; refract out; dispersion.

**Caption idea:** Rainbow path inside a raindrop.

## 043_Prism_06.png — Newton split and recombine

## 043_Prism_07.png — Total internal reflection

**Placement:** End of “Total Internal Reflection.”

**Scene:** Glass prism; ray inside glass hits slanted face at steep angle; reflects inside (dashed normal optional) instead of exiting. Contrast: shallower ray that escapes (optional small inset).

**Labels:** Total internal reflection; light stays in glass.

**Caption idea:** Total internal reflection inside a prism.

## 043_Prism_08.png — Binocular light path

**Caption idea:** Prisms fold the light path in binoculars.
